Drew Templeton


My name is Drew Templeton and I am a Marketer, Web Developer, Graphic Designer, Videographer, Photographer and Entrepreneur. (Actually starting companies, not just using it as a buzzword on LinkedIn)

I graduated from the University of Missouri in 2008 with my B.S. in Psychology and from DePaul University with my M.A. in Public Relations & Advertising in 2011. Having an educational background that tailors to how and why people think and act they way they do allows me to create work that is custom fitted for that specific target audience.

One of my main passions is playing professional paintball. Yes, paintball. Most people tell me they didn't know that existed. I've been lucky enough to have traveled all over the world for the past sixteen years of my life to compete at the highest level of competition.
