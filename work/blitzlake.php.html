BlitzLake

www.blitzlake.com

Project Type: Web Design & Development

Scope- BlitzLake Capital needed a website designed and developed that professionally portrayed themselves and showcased their impressive portfolio.

Designing a site for a private equity firm can sound daunting and a little bit boring, but BlitzLake was ambitious with the expectations for their online personality.

Design

With the site being very content driven, the layout needed to cater to maximum legibility, while the design elements needed to promote the company's sense of professionalism.

The layout of the site is a one column layout with "chunky" text. No super-thin-Ã¼ber-cool sans-serif fonts for this project. Myriad Pro was chosen for it's classy, yet modern features, a personality trait that BlitzLake is after.

BlitzLake's Website - Tablet View

A subtle color palette, driven by a soothing blue color was chosen. The color blue plays a role in the psychology of the viewer, promoting a sense of security and stability. With viewers looking into BlitzLake's multi-million dollar portfolio, stability is one of the main things you're looking for.

Although the typographical elements are the showcase for the content driven site, photographs of iconic Chicago scenery were used to promote a familiarity with the readers, BlitzLake, and their city.

Iconic Chicago Scenery

Development

BlitzLake's site had to be on top of it's cross-browser compatibility. With visitors potentially investing a boat load of money with the company, if the site layout breaks, it doesn't speak very highly of the company. Modernizr was used to detect browser compatibilty with the animated elements on the site, as well the scroll effects.

BlitzLake's Website - Mobile View

Even with minimal mobile traffic, BlitzLake still incorporates a responsive design.

BlitzLake didn't ask for a repsonsive site, they just asked for a site that's impressive. Responsive design allows for those few people that do view the site on their iPad or their Galaxy S4 to enjoy their experience on the site. It's experiences like those that boost the credibilty of the company and gives me the pat on the back for a job well done.

The site features a CSS only mobile slide out menu which reduces the dependency of Javascript on mobile devices and results in some buttery-smooth animations.
